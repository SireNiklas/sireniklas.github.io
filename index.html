Skycadia

I joined the team developing Skycadia, as the gameplay engineer intern and was assigned to gameplay mechanics. I was tasked to profile issues, create new weapons, implement a ship and pilot stats modifier system using Scriptable Objects. Everything was done whilst writing modular and flexible code. I was the subject matter expert of git and familiarized my fellow interns with its concepts and applications.

Magical Horde Shooter

Progression

Multiplayer Prototype

Magical Horde Shooter Started as a game sandbox to play around and learn the Unity Game Engine. The preview is its current state that involves. Custom Player Movment, Cinemachine Camera, Unity's New Input System, Navmesh AI Movement, Raycasts, UI.

Projectile Fight

Projectile Fight, my second attempt at a multiplayer game. It is a work in progress multiplayer FPS, planned to be released on Steam. I have developed it using Facepunch Steamworks, and Unity's Netcode for Gameobjects. Currently it supports steam lobbies, servers, and simple FPS play including death and shooting. Due to it being in very early stages of development, there is little to show.

Other Protoypes
